MealPool

Bring one meal a week to work, eat four days for free.

Why MealPool?

When eating at work, your options are to eat out, or bring a meal every day. Eating out is costly and bringing meals becomes tedious and boring. With MealPool, you can eat different dishes each day, and best of all, you only have to bring food to work one day.

How does it work?

You can joing a meal pool near your place of work or study. Each person in your pool is responsible for bringing a meal for the group each week. You can choose 3 meals that you would like to bring on your assigned day and your pool mates can vote and choose the dish they prefer.
